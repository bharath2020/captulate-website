Captulate Support

Welcome to Captulate!

Captulate helps you instantly calculate mathematical expressions from any area of your screen. Simply select a region containing numbers, and Captulate will extract and evaluate them for you.

Key Features

  • Global hotkey (Cmd+Shift+C) for instant capture
  • Smart OCR text recognition for numerical extraction
  • Live calculation preview
  • Menu bar integration for easy access
  • Multi-monitor support

Frequently Asked Questions

How do I grant screen recording permission?

When you first use Captulate, macOS will prompt you to grant screen recording permission. You can also enable it manually:

  1. Open System Settings
  2. Go to Privacy & Security → Screen Recording
  3. Toggle on Captulate
  4. Restart Captulate if needed

What's the keyboard shortcut?

Press Cmd+Shift+C to start capturing. You can also click the Captulate icon in your menu bar.

What types of calculations are supported?

Captulate supports:

  • Basic arithmetic (+, -, ×, ÷)
  • Percentages
  • Currency calculations
  • Multi-line calculations
  • Mixed number formats

Why isn't text being recognized?

For best results:

  • Ensure text is clearly visible and not too small
  • Select a tight area around the numbers
  • Avoid selecting areas with complex backgrounds
  • Make sure screen recording permission is granted

How do I quit Captulate?

Click the Captulate icon in your menu bar and select "Quit Captulate".

Privacy & Security

Captulate processes all calculations locally on your Mac. No screen content or personal data is ever transmitted or stored. For details, see our Privacy Policy.
